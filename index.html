About

Banking discipline, built teller by teller.

I work the front line of banking — where cash, compliance and customers meet every single day. At Afghan United Bank's Herat Main Branch I manage deposits, withdrawals, MoneyGram, domestic and telegraphic transfers, and daily cash balancing, all under strict AML and KYC controls.

I'm currently completing my Bachelor's in Economics and Management at Jami University, building the analytical side to match the operational one.
